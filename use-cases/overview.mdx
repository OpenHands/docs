## What Makes a Good Use Case

The best tasks for OpenHands are:

<CardGroup cols={2}>

## Best Practices

For the best results:

1. **Start small**: Begin with simple, well-defined tasks
2. **Provide context**: Include relevant background information
3. **Set clear criteria**: Define what "done" looks like
4. **Verify results**: Always review and test the output
5. **Iterate**: Refine your approach based on results
